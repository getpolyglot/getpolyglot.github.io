What is Polyglot?

Connecting People!

Polyglot is a communication server that enables you to efficiently communicate with your coworkers or friends. All data is stored with the server, so no third party has access to your conversations!

Chat with many services in one place!

Polyglot's built-in bridging feature allows you to take part in conversations taking place on other platforms without a difference. After authorizing your account, your conversations and groupchats are transparently proxied by Polyglot to look just like regular local chats.

Connect every device, no matter how old!

Polyglot is built on the rock-solid foundations of XMPP, the tried and tested instant messaging protocol. Thanks to that, you can use Polyglot from anything that has an XMPP client, from a 30 year old PC to the lastest iPhone.

Just how you want it!

Don't have infrastructure? We'll provide a cloud instance for all your needs.
Have a server to spare? With our docker image and an Ansible playbook, the setup will be a breeze.
